About the Role

TechNova is looking for an experienced Full-Stack Engineer to join our growing platform team. You'll work on high-impact features that are used by thousands of businesses daily — building everything from customer-facing dashboards to backend API infrastructure.

What You'll Do

  • Design and build scalable backend services using Node.js and PostgreSQL
  • Develop clean, performant React frontends with a focus on UX quality
  • Collaborate with Product and Design on new feature development

  • Mentor junior engineers and conduct thorough code reviews

Requirements

  • 5+ years of professional software engineering experience

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

  • Unlimited PTO with a recommended minimum of 20 days
  • Home office setup stipend ($1,500)
